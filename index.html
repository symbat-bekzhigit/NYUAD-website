Welcome , dear friend .

Here, I present a glipmse of a slow day in my life at NYUAD.
By “slow” I mean a day when I choose to appreciate the beauty of our campus in all its form and shape.
Its silent beauty, when it is not crowded with students rushing from one building to another.
A day when I observe, notice, and reflect.

When was the last time you stopped to appreciate the birds chipping around campus?
Or how the sun glistens on the highline grass after the grass has been watered?
If you do not remember it or cannot recall, watch the video below and look through my gallery of photos of our beautiful campus.

Hope it will encourage you to be more present and appreciate the beauty of simple things.
Best,
Symbat
